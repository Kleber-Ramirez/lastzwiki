Wrench System

To modify and upgrade your armament, you need wrenches. Each level requires a specific number of wrenches divided into sub-levels.

Upon reaching certain levels, you will unlock special vehicles such as the Cheetah, Hercules, Destroyer and Destroyer-EX. The Conqueror is obtained for free by completing the Workshop tutorial missions.

📖 How the System Works

🔢 Sub-levels

Each level is divided into sub-levels (usually 5, 8 or 10 depending on the level). You must complete all of them to advance to the next level.

Wrenches per Sub-level

Each sub-level requires a certain number of wrenches. E.g.: Lv20 = 2 wrenches × 5 sub-levels = 10 total wrenches for that level.

📊 Accumulated Total

The "Accumulated" column shows the total wrenches spent from the start. To reach Lv240 you need 70,700 wrenches in total.

💡 Modification Tips

  • The Conqueror is free — complete the Workshop tutorial missions to get it from Lv0
  • Prioritize reaching Lv45 to unlock the Cheetah — you only need 150 accumulated wrenches
  • The biggest jump is between Lv145 and Lv240 — almost 60,000 extra wrenches for the Destroyer-EX
  • Plan your progression according to the armament you need most for your alliance strategy
  • Save wrenches for the key levels: Lv45, Lv95, Lv145 and Lv195
  • Events often give wrenches — take full advantage of them to progress without spending resources

🔑 Golden Keys System

To modify and upgrade your armament, you need golden keys. Each level requires a specific number of keys divided into sub-levels.

Upon reaching certain levels, you will unlock special vehicles such as the Cheetah, Hercules, Double Cannons, Snowplow and Destroyer.

📖 How the System Works

🔢 Sub-levels

Each level is divided into sub-levels (usually 5, 8 or 10 depending on the level). You must complete all of them to advance to the next level.

🔑 Keys per Sub-level

Each sub-level requires a certain number of golden keys. E.g.: level 20 = 2 keys × 5 sub-levels = 10 total keys for that level.

📊 Accumulated Total

The "Accumulated" column shows the total keys spent from the start. To reach level 145 you need 10,270 keys in total.

💡 Modification Tips

  • Prioritize reaching level 20 to unlock the Cheetah — you only need 30 keys
  • The biggest jump is between level 105 and 145 (almost 8,000 extra keys)
  • Plan your progression according to the armament you need most for your strategy
  • Save golden keys for the key levels: 20, 70, 105, 130 and 145
  • Events often give golden keys — take full advantage of them

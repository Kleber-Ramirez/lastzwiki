Which buildings to upgrade first in Last Z: Survival Shooter?

⚠️ This guide reflects my personal experience and is based on the total power each building provides. Every building serves an important role in the game — this ranking helps you know which ones to focus on first. To see requirements, levels and details for each building visit the Buildings page.

A Very important — High priority
B Important — Upgrade when you can
C Secondary — No rush

💡 Game Tips & Tricks

Learn key strategies to dominate Last Z: Survival Shooter

⚡ Mobility

Quick Reinforcement

If there's an alliance treasure far away, you can get there faster by reinforcing allied cities or bases. This reinforcement speed only works within your alliance territory.

⚔️ Combat

Boost Your Formation's Attack

Align 5 heroes of the same faction in each troop to get ATK +115%. Prioritize Bloody Rose in your first formation.

🎯 Strategy

Armor Rotation in Caravan

Transfer your best armor between formations to maximize rounds in Caravan. Pass equipment from Rose → Dawn → Guardian as you progress.

🎉 Events

Maximize Events

In events like Full Preparation, save resources for the themes that give the most points. Don't spend everything on the first day of the event.

💰 Economy

Save Resource Boxes

Resource boxes increase in value based on your HQ level. Save the golden boxes and use them after level 27+ to get more resources.

Use Alliance Buffs

Donate to your alliance to unlock technologies that benefit everyone. Prioritize construction and research buffs at the start.

💵 Purchases

Best Purchases for Beginners

If you're going to spend money, prioritize Sophia ($1) for her construction ability and the 2nd Builder ($2) to speed up your early progress.

🔍 Reconnaissance

Locate Scouted Bases

When you receive or see a shared reconnaissance report, click the coordinates below the player's name to go directly to their location on the map.

⚕️ Hospital

Efficient Healing with Alliance Help

Instead of healing all your wounded troops at once, heal in small batches based on how many active members are in your alliance. You'll save time and speedups.

🤝 Alliance

Fast Donation in Technologies

Instead of clicking repeatedly to donate, hold down the button. This activates multipliers (x2, x5, x10) to donate faster and earn more points.

Automatic Troop Return

Before attacking, enable the "Auto Return" checkbox. Your troops will automatically return after combat without having to do it manually.

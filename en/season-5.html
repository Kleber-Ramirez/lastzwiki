📋 Introduction

Season 5: Moonlit Elegy brings a vampire theme to Last Z. Like Season 3 (Ice) and Season 4 (Dinosaurs), the core mechanic remains the same: season buildings, defeating monsters and glory wars — but with a completely new setting and enemies.

In S5 you will face Werewolves and Succubus/Knight Scubus, two monster types with different fuel costs and rewards. The Mysterious Witch and Moonlight Collection systems also debut — a new card mechanic granting temporary buffs to your troops.

🎯 Key Features:

  • 🐺 New monsters: Werewolves (8 fuel) and Succubus/Knight Scubus (16 fuel)
  • 🏗️ Exclusive buildings with 2 unique resources: Holy Water and Shadow Blood Crystal
  • 🔮 Mysterious Witch system — leveled up with Demon Stones
  • 🃏 Moonlight Collection — 52 cards with troop buffs (season only)
  • 🌙 Nightfall event — grows 1 hour per week until eternal night (week 8)
  • 🏪 Trading Stations return (available day 11)
  • ⚔️ Awakening system for Oliveira, Scarlett and Evely
  • 🎓 Mastery up to level 90 (previously 70)

🧪 Serum Laboratory PRIORITY #1

Function: Grants Virus Resistance, allowing you to attack higher-level Succubus and Werewolves.

Why is it the most important? Without enough virus resistance, you cannot attack high-level monsters. Higher-level monsters = more Blood Crystal = more building upgrades.

Strategy: Upgrade it constantly throughout the season. It is the main bottleneck of S5.

💧 Shadow Spring PRIORITY #2

Function: Mines Holy Water, one of the two resources needed to upgrade buildings.

Strategy: Second priority. Higher level = more passive Holy Water production.

🎯 Command Center

Function: Increases your troops' DAMAGE in season glory.

When to upgrade: After Serum Lab and Shadow Spring are at a good level.

🔥 Fire Control Center

Function: Increases your troops' ATTACK in season.

When to upgrade: Together with Command Center to maximize offense.

🛡️ Defensive Fortress

Function: Increases your troops' DEFENSE in season glory.

When to upgrade: For players focused on withstanding attacks during wars.

⚠️ IMPORTANT: As in previous seasons, individual rewards will be given based on season resources accumulated at the end. Manage Holy Water and Blood Crystal wisely.

💎 Exclusive Season Resources

S5 introduces two unique resources needed to upgrade season buildings. Both are critical — you need both simultaneously.

💧 Holy Water

Source: Mined passively at the Shadow Spring.

Use: Required to upgrade all season buildings.

Strategy: Upgrade Shadow Spring constantly to increase production.

🩸 Shadow Blood Crystal

Source: Obtained by defeating Werewolves and Succubus.

Use: Together with Holy Water, needed to upgrade buildings.

Strategy: Maximize your daily monsters to accumulate Blood Crystal.

🪨 Demon Stone

Source: Golden chest upon getting the first kill on a monster.

Use: Delivered to the Mysterious Witch to raise her affection level.

Strategy: Make sure to get the first kill on at least one monster per day.

🌙 Moon Token

Source: When you obtain a Moonlight Card you already have, it automatically converts into Moon Tokens.

Use: Currency for the Night Market at Trading Stations.

Strategy: Save them for the best Night Market deals.

🐺 Season Monsters

S5 features two monster types. Each consumes different amounts of fuel and grants different rewards.

🔥 8 combustible

🐺 Werewolf

Difficulty: More accessible — ideal for players still upgrading the Serum Lab.

Reward: Shadow Blood Crystal + first kill grants Demon Stone.

Strategy: Attack them while your virus resistance is not yet enough for high-level Succubus.

🔥 16 combustible

😈 Succubus / Knight Scubus

Difficulty: Higher — requires more virus resistance (high Serum Lab).

Reward: Greater amount of Blood Crystal + first kill gives Demon Stone.

Nightfall Event: During Nightfall, special Knight Scubus appear for team raids. Ranking is based on the highest level defeated.

⚠️ Important: Only the player who starts the rally and kills the Scubus during Nightfall receives ranking — not participants.

🌙 Nightfall Event

Nightfall is the central combat event of S5. It appears during Apocalypse Time hours:

  • ⏰ Appears at 08:00 and 16:00 Apocalypse Time each day
  • 📈 Growing duration: starts at 1 hour in week 1, increases 1 hour each week
  • 🌑 Week 8: the world enters a state of eternal night
  • 🏆 Weekly ranking based on the highest level Knight Scubus defeated
  • 👥 Only the player who starts the rally and scores the kill receives a ranking position
💡 Nightfall TIP: Coordinate with your alliance to launch rallies against Knight Scubus during Nightfall windows. The player who starts the rally and kills the monster is the one who climbs the ranking — choose wisely who leads to maximize alliance ranking.

👹 Furious Boss: Serverus

The new furious boss of S5 is Serverus, a hellhound with a vampire theme. It works the same as previous season furious bosses — appears on the map and can attack bases.

🚧 Daily monster limit: In previous seasons the limit was 30 the first week and 15 after. For S5 this data is still to be confirmed. We will update it as soon as we have verified information.

🔮 The Mysterious Witch

One of the new systems in S5. The Mysterious Witch works similarly to the Snowman (S3) or Dinosaur (S4): you level her up to unlock rewards and buffs.

⚙️ How does it work?

  1. Obtain Demon Stones — earned as first kill reward when defeating monsters
  2. Deliver the stones to the Witch to increase her affection level
  3. Higher affection level unlocks Moon Watchers — the currency to obtain cards
  4. Exchange Moon Watchers for Moonlight Card draws

🔓 Key Unlock Levels:

🌙 Level 3 — Moonlight Watcher

You can start exchanging Moon Watchers for Moonlight Cards.

🃏 Level 10 — Moonlight Blessing

Unlocks the moonlight blessing system to equip cards to your troops.

⚡ Level 21 — Additional Slots

More slots unlocked to equip cards simultaneously.

✨ Level 33 — 12 Active Cards

All 12 slots enabled to take full advantage of buffs at 100%.

💡 Strategy: Prioritize getting the first kill on monsters every day to accumulate Demon Stones. Reaching level 10 of the Witch as soon as possible unlocks the blessing system and lets you get more value from cards early on.

🃏 Moonlight Collection

The card system is completely new in S5. There are 52 cards in total that you can equip to your troops to get buffs during the season.

⚠️ Important: Moonlight Card effects are season only. When S5 ends, all buffs are automatically removed.

🎴 Card Buff Types:

  • 🛡️ Troop Defense
  • ⚔️ Troop Attack
  • ❤️ Troop HP
  • 💥 Troop Damage

⭐ Card Rarities:

🥇

Golden

Highest buff. Hardest to obtain. Top priority in draws.

🟣

Purple

Intermediate buff. Good balance between accessibility and effectiveness.

🔵

Blue

Minor buff. Easiest to obtain. Useful for filling slots.

🔄 Duplicate System — Moon Tokens

If you obtain a card you already have, it automatically converts into Moon Tokens. These tokens are the currency of the Night Market at Trading Stations, where you can buy rare items.

⚙️ How to Equip Cards:

Each card can only be in one slot at a time. You can equip them in specific troop slots to add the corresponding buff to that troop, or to increase the global blessing effect.

💡 Strategy: Prioritize golden cards that improve the attack and damage of your main formation. Slots are unlocked by leveling up the Mysterious Witch — reach level 33 to have all 12 slots active.

🦸 Heroes and Awakening System

S5 introduces the Awakening System for SS season heroes. Instead of recruitment tickets as in previous seasons, ranking prizes are now S5 Awakening Badges.

🌟 Heroes with Awakening in S5:

⚔️ Oliveira

SS season hero. Awakening requires accumulating S5 awakening badges. You need 5 stars before you can awaken.

🗡️ Scarlett

Second SS season hero. Like Oliveira, the awakening system requires badges accumulated from season ranking.

✨ Evely

Third hero with awakening option in S5. Awakening badges are universal — you can choose which of the three to apply them to.

🏆 Ranking Rewards — Awakening Badges:

  • 🥇 Individual Rank 1: 80 S5 awakening badge chests
  • 🤝 Alliance rank 21–50: minimum 10 S5 awakening badge chests
  • 🤝 Alliance rank 11–20: minimum 10 S5 awakening badge chests
💡 Note: The exact number of badges needed for awakening has not yet been revealed by the developers. We will update it when confirmed.

🎓 Mastery Hall — Up to Level 90

S5 raises the mastery limit from 70 to 90. Levels 1–70 are the same as S4. Levels 75–90 bring new abilities.

🤝 Mastery: SUPPORT

Recommended for: Most players. Maximizes individual progress.

📚 Research Reduction

Reduces research time in all queues by 30 minutes. Higher level = greater reduction.

⚡ Research Speed Boost

Increases Laboratory research speed.

🎯 Double Attempt — Missions

When obtaining an S rank or higher bounty mission, 15% chance to receive double rewards.

🛒 VIP Limit +50%

Increases the purchase limit in the VIP Shop by 50% — more badges, speedups and wrenches.

📅 Calendar Swap

Allows swapping the Preparedness Calendar theme once every 5 days. Get more chips of the type you need most.

⚔️ Mastery: COMBAT

Recommended for: Active PvP and war players. Only if you are already above level 30.

🔄 Battle Regrouping

If your troops lose a field battle, they regroup on the spot for 2 seconds — surviving units are healed and reenter combat. Ideal for big spenders.

💀 Casualty Reduction

Reduces perished troops by 1% during city sieges.

🚛 Instant Convoy

Once per day, instantly delivers an active convoy to its destination. Very useful if you have valuable items on the way.

🧟 Zombie Siege Damage +3%

Increases troop damage by 3% during the Zombie Siege event.

💡 Recommendation: If you are below level 30, you will be Support by default. From level 30 you can choose. For most F2P players, Support remains the best option due to research reduction and VIP shop benefits.

When I started playing Last Z, I made the classic mistake: I invested fragments equally into every hero I owned. After a month, I realized my B-rank heroes were completely useless in my main formation — I had wasted resources that could have gone into my S heroes. I completely changed my strategy.

The #1 rule I learned: focus on your 3 strongest S heroes from your main faction and leave the 2 weakest at base level. You'll replace them next season anyway. The faction order is: S1 Blood Rose → S2 Wings of Dawn → S3 Order Guard.

🏰 The 3 Factions & Their Season Order

🌹 Blood Rose — Season 1

The first faction. Lysia + Bella together activate one of the strongest combos — increased damage resistance against Assault units. Sofia is essential for new players: her skill 3 passively reduces construction time even when not in combat. If you're just starting, focus here.

🕊️ Wings of Dawn — Season 2

Long-range shooters. Alma and Scarlett are pillars — both have exclusive gear that keeps them competitive in Season 3. Laura is the only reliable free frontline. Emilia's global research passive saves Laboratory resources.

🛡️ Order Guard — Season 3

The Biker faction. Chinatsu is valuable in Alliance Versus events for duel points. Halina is the best S2 frontline — very tanky with good Biker damage.

📊 Tier System: What Each Rank Means

S Tier

Most powerful heroes. Very high base stats and passives that affect the whole formation. Invest heavy fragments here — especially skill 3. Most obtained via season pass or special events.

A Tier

Transition heroes — useful early but replaced once you complete your S team. Universal A fragments are perfect for secondary formations.

B Tier

Useful early for resource gathering. Once all skills maxed, Blue Manuals can be recycled for resources and even S fragments.

⚠️ Important — Skill 3 "Active in Expeditions": Some heroes have skill 3 labeled "Active in Expeditions" — the buff only applies when deployed. Others say "Effective on all troops" — applies even if not in team. Affected: Lysia, Bella, Selena, Nyx, Alma, Sakura, Halina, Chinatsu.

💡 What I Learned About Hero Investment

🎯

Rule of the 3 S heroes: Focus all fragments on your 3 strongest S heroes. The 2 weakest will be replaced next season.

📈

Prioritize Skill 3: For supports like Sofia, Oliveira, Emilia, Laura, Mia — skill 3 is a global passive that works even when not in combat.

⏸️

Don't waste EXP on A or B heroes: Save all experience to instantly level up S heroes when you unlock next HQ level.

🔮

Plan with season vision: When a new S hero arrives, evaluate if they fit your main faction. If not, wait for your faction's season.

💎 How to Get S Hero Fragments as F2P

Increase VIP level for extra missions. Daily truck robberies/sends give fragments. Season events reward S fragments. Most importantly: alliance ranking at end of season — leader distributes diamonds and exclusive tickets to recruit S heroes you didn't buy. Being in a strong, active alliance is key.

♻️ Advanced Tip: Recycle Your Blue Skill Manuals from B Heroes

Once all B heroes have max skills, Blue Manuals pile up. Go to Warehouse → Hero tab → find Blue Skill Manuals → select "Recycle". Each manual turns into a Random Basic Resource Box that can give wood, electricity, food, Z Coins, diamonds, or even S-rank hero fragments. Don't waste them.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Which hero is best for beginners?
Jack (Wings of Dawn) is easy to get and has good ranged damage. Pair with Alexander as tank.
Is it worth upgrading B-rank heroes?
As temporary filler until you get S copies. Later recycle their skill manuals.
How to get hero fragments quickly?
Daily missions, weekly events, alliance shop, truck robberies/sends.
Which skill should I upgrade first?
Ultimate (skill 4) first, then passive (skill 3). Basic skills last.
Pure faction or mixed team?
Pure faction gives +15% stat bonus, ideal for endgame. Mixed is more flexible early.

🔑 How to Redeem Codes

Method 1 — From the Official Store

  1. Ve a store.last-z.com and log in
  2. Click the ☰ menu (top left corner)
  3. Select "Gift Code Redemption"
  4. Enter the code and confirm
  5. Rewards will be sent to your in-game mail

Method 2 — Gift Center

  1. Ve a last-z.com/giftCenter
  2. Enter your UID and click LOGIN
  3. Type the code in "Enter Gift Code"
  4. Click CONFIRM
  5. Rewards will be sent to your in-game mail
❓ Don't know your UID? In the Gift Center, click the ? icon next to the UID field. You'll see a 3-step tutorial:
1. Tap your Avatar (top left corner of the game)
2. Tap Settings at the bottom
3. Tap "Copy ID"

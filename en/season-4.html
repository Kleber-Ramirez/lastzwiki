🦕 Season 4: Dinosaur Origin

The prehistoric world has arrived in Last Z. Face dinosaurs, dominate the Jurassic jungle and raise your own legend.

⏱️ 91 days (13 weeks) 🗺️ Jurassic Jungle Map 🦖 Velociraptors + Stegosaurus 💎 Max specialization level: 70

📋 Introduction

Season 4 is the longest and most complex yet — 91 days (13 weeks). The map transforms into a prehistoric Jurassic jungle. Zombies disappear and are replaced by dinosaurs. There are more events, new systems, and greater strategic depth than any previous season.

⚠️ F2P WARNING: S4 is less free-to-play friendly than S3, mainly due to Trade Hubs requiring diamonds. It is strongly recommended to save diamonds before entering S4.

🎯 What's New in S4:

  • Brand new map — green jungle, Jurassic Park theme
  • 3 new heroes: Yu Chan, Queenie and Amber
  • New system: Hatch of Legends (raise a dinosaur)
  • New system: Trade Hubs (commerce hubs)
  • New event: Ancient Dino (massive boss every Sunday)
  • New event: Dino Rescue + Black Market Guardian
  • New: Fossil Album (collection of 20 fossils)
  • New: Hero Level Swap (level exchange system)
  • Specialization extended to level 70

You can swap hero levels and skill levels between two heroes permanently. This allows you to transfer all investment from an old hero to a new S4 hero without losing progress — one of the most powerful systems ever added to the game.

⚔️ Power (level 60+)

🧬 Biotech Lab PRIORIDAD #1

Function: Increases your virus resistance — determines which level of dinosaurs you can attack.

Why it's critical: Higher dinosaur level = more glory points, more badges, better rewards. Without high virus resistance, you are locked out of good rewards.

When to stop: Once you can defeat level 60 dinosaurs, upgrading it is no longer mandatory for progression.

🌿 Bio Greenhouse PRIORIDAD #2

Function: Produces Fern Leaves — the main resource for upgrading buildings.

Quantity: 4 by default. The 5th unlocks with Wartime Investment.

Strategy: Build all 4 from the very start of the season.

⚔️ Command Center

Function: Increases seasonal troop damage.

When to upgrade: Only after Biotech Lab and Bio Greenhouse are at a good level.

🛡️ Armory (x2)

Function: Increases seasonal troop attack.

Note: You can build up to 2. A third unlocks with Combat specialization level 60+.

🏰 Fortification (x2)

Function: Increases seasonal troop defense.

Note: Also up to 2 units. Upgrade alongside Armory to balance offense/defense.

🏯 Alliance Centers (x3)

Function: Required for Glory War and alliance progression. Redesigned with dinosaur theme.

Strategy: Place them as early as possible — teamwork is essential.

⚠️ COMMON MISTAKE: Do not rush Command Center, Armory or Fortification early. They are useful, but virus resistance comes first. Without it, you cannot attack good-level dinosaurs.

📦 Key Resources:

🌿 Fern Leaves

Produced in Bio Greenhouses. Main resource for upgrading all seasonal buildings.

⚖️ Cretaceous Scales

Obtained by killing NPCs on the map. Secondary resource equally critical for buildings.

🦖 NPCs and Raid System

The map changes completely. Zombies are replaced by dinosaurs. You have a limit of 15 daily rewards total — prioritize correctly.

🗺️ Map NPCs:

🦖 Velociraptors PRIORITY #1

Equivalent to: Regular zombies from previous seasons.

Reward: Cretaceous Scales + glory points.

Strategy: Your main daily source. Always attack the highest level you can.

🦕 Stegosauruses PRIORITY #2

Equivalent to: Boomers from previous seasons.

Reward: Ancient Dino Eggs + Cretaceous Scales.

Limit: 15 daily rewards (shared with Velociraptors).

🦅 Predator Squads

Max level: 200 on the map.

Rewards: Seasonal resources, Ancient Dino Eggs, Blueprints, Hero EXP, Pine Cones.

Limit: 2 kills/day (Monday–Saturday) · 5 kills on Sundays.

🦣 Black Market Guardian DOMINGO

Max level: 350 — requires rally.

When: Sundays only — Dino Rescue event.

Ranking: Based on damage dealt. State achievements give rewards to everyone.

🏆 Glory System:

Killing dinosaurs generates Glory Points. Higher level dinosaurs give more glory. Every 8 hours you can collect a Glory Chest:

  • Without Season Journey: up to 148 badges per chest
  • With Season Journey: rewards doubled → ~300 badges every 8h → ~900 badges/day
  • There is no ranking reward on the glory leaderboard — what matters is progressing faster to earn more badges sooner
  • If you can't defeat high-level dinosaurs alone → join rallies from stronger players and you'll still earn glory
💡 Bones & Eggs Boom: A permanent bonus that unlocks mid-season. Once active, it lasts until the end and doubles Cretaceous Scales and Ancient Dino Eggs obtained. Makes mid-game progression much faster.

🌴 Season 4 Special Events

🦖 Ancient Dino — Todos los Domingos

Hora: 12:00 Hora Apocalipsis.

Everyone attacks a massive dinosaur with no attack limit. The top 3 damage dealers on the first kill receive a unique title.

5 Ancient Dinos: T-Rex, Triceratops, Perasaur, Ankylosaurs, Demitradon.

Participation rewards: Fossils, Tactical Plug-in Chests, speedups.

⚠️ The event lasts only minutes — very easy to miss.

🥚 Dino Rescue + Black Market Guardian

Cuándo: Domingos.

The Black Market Guardian (max level 350) appears and requires a rally.

State achievements give rewards to all players: exclusive equipment fragments, orange hero fragments, tickets, fuel and Dino Feed.

State-wide cooperation is key.

🛡️ Dino Egg Defense

Similar to Zombie Siege Defense. You defend your HQ against waves of dinosaur attacks.

Priority: completion — completion rewards matter more than ranking.

Max completion reward: 14 Golden Wrenches.

🏪 Trade Hubs — Mon/Wed/Fri

Hora: 11:00 Hora Apocalipsis.

3 hub levels. First week: state only. Then: cross-state competition begins.

Alliances capture hubs, generate diamond tax envelopes and unlock exclusive shops.

Sells: Badges, exclusive fragments, plug-in chests, speedups, steel, power cores — all purchases require diamonds.

📖 Fossil Album

Collect 20 different fossils. Obtained in the Ancient Dino event.

Complete album: Orange hero fragments, exclusive equipment fragments, Ancient Dino Eggs and more.

Fósiles duplicados se canjean por Huevos de Dino Antiguo (Ancient Dino Eggs).

🏬 Bio Institute Shop

Opens: Saturdays and Sundays only.

Se compra con Huevos de Dino Antiguo (Ancient Dino Eggs). Vende fragmentos de equipo exclusivo, fragmentos héroe naranja, badges, speedups, recursos y fósiles.

14 refreshes per weekend — plan carefully what to buy.

🥚 Hatch of Legends — Cría tu Dinosaurio:

One of the most unique systems in S4. Choose one species and raise it to level 30.

🦖 T-Rex

One of the 3 available dinosaurs. Gives the same bonuses as the others.

🦕 Triceratops

Second option. Choose based on your aesthetic preference.

🦴 Ankylosaurs

Third option. Same bonus as the others.

  • Each level = +1% Troop Attack → maximum +30% at level 30
  • At level 16 → unlocks an exclusive vehicle skin for that species
  • You can only choose one species per season
  • Fed with Dino Feed and Pine Cones — earned through tasks and killing NPCs
  • Very free-to-play friendly system

⚔️ Glory War:

Your state is grouped with 3 other states for S4. Wars happen every Tuesday. Alliances fight for season points — higher rankings = better rewards including S4 recruitment tickets.

🎓 Specialization Room (Max Level 70)

S4 extends specialization to level 70. S3 skills remain up to level 50. From level 51 onwards there are new S4-exclusive skills.

🔀 Choice System:

  • Level <30: SUPPORT by default.
  • Level 30+: Choose between SUPPORT or COMBAT.
💡 S4 Start Recommendation: Switch to SUPPORT. It gives faster construction, more seasonal resources and extra virus resistance from Bio Greenhouses — a huge early advantage.

🤝 Specialization: SUPPORT

Recommended for: Everyone at the start of S4. Maximizes early progress and helps the team.

— New S4 skills (level 51–70) —

📦 +2% basic resources when defeating NPCs/Boomers/monsters

Very useful throughout the season. More resources from each dinosaur killed.

⭐ +2% mastery EXP when defeating NPCs (upgradeable x5 → up to +10%)

Speeds up specialization level progression.

🏗️ Reduce construction time of all queues -30 min (upgradeable x5)

Can stack up to potentially 2-3 hours saved across all queues simultaneously.

⚡ Construction speed boost

Overall construction speed increased.

🏥 Hospital capacity boost (you + ally)

+5% hospital capacity for both for 4 hours. Not stackable but renewable.

🚜 Gathering speed bonus

Gather map resources faster.

🔓 Max assignable mastery points +5

Allows assigning 5 extra points to other specialization paths.

⚔️ Specialization: COMBAT

Recommended for: Level 30+ players focused on war with high hero power.

— New S4 skills (level 51–70) —

⚡ 2 Free Advanced Teleports

1 energy point every 72h, max 2 stacked. Teleport to anywhere on the map — no need for nearby allies.

🔁 Instant troop recall (daily use, upgradeable x2)

Returns a troop to your HQ instantly regardless of distance.

🏃 Rally attacks can depart early

Launch rallies instantly (normally 3-5 min wait). Resets every Monday — use it wisely.

🛡️ Defending HQ: troop attack + defense +2%

Ideal for players with a defensive strategy.

🏗️ Build one extra defensive structure

Permanently unlocks one additional seasonal defensive building.

🔫 Armory building cap +1

Allows building a third Armory for more troop attack.

💥 Troop damage boost (field combat, looting trucks, canyon)

Multiple combat situations with increased damage.

🏆 Season Point Ranking

Alliance Season Points = Personal Glory (all members) + City Points

📊 How to Earn Points:

  • Personal Glory: Actively participating in battles and seasonal events.
  • Cities: Occupying a city generates points over time. Accumulated points are NOT lost when you lose the city.
  • Glory War: Winning allows you to steal points from the enemy (every Tuesday).

💡 Tips to Maximize Progress

🌱 Season Start:

  • ✅ Build all 4 Bio Greenhouses from day one
  • ✅ Prioritize the Biotech Lab above everything else
  • ✅ Switch to SUPPORT specialization for early advantage
  • ✅ Place the 3 Alliance Centers as early as possible with your alliance
  • ✅ Save diamonds — Trade Hubs require them

🏃 Mid-Season:

  • ✅ Join rallies if you can't defeat high-level dinosaurs alone
  • ✅ Participate in Ancient Dino every Sunday to collect fossils
  • ✅ Progress in the Fossil Album — the rewards are excellent
  • ✅ Una vez en nivel 60, diversifica recursos en Command Center, Armory y Fortification
  • ✅ Take advantage of Bones & Eggs Boom when it activates

🏁 Late Season:

  • ✅ Prioritize Trade Hubs — badges and exclusive fragments first
  • ✅ Complete Season Journey to double glory chests
  • ✅ Actively participate in Glory War — it defines your final reward
  • ✅ Use Hero Level Swap to maximize your new S4 heroes

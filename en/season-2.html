🏜️ Season 2: Desert Guardian

The desert has arrived in Last Z. Conquer resource plots, dominate the oasis and lead your alliance to victory.

⏱️ ~80 days 🗺️ Desert Map 🌵 Zones 1–320 💎 Max building level: 30

⚠️ From zone 321 onwards, Season 2 is Ice-themed.

🇪🇸 Versión en Español →

📋 Introduction

Season 2 — Desert Guardian — introduces a completely different mechanic from the base: resource plots. Instead of boss raids, the focus is on conquering and holding plots of Desert Heart and Blue Crystal on the map.

🎯 Main Features:

  • Resource plot system on the desert map
  • Two exclusive resources: 🌵 Desert Heart and 💎 Blue Crystal (levels 1-14)
  • 6 exclusive season buildings (maximum level 30)
  • Specialization system: Support vs Combat (levels 1-30)
  • Plots can be stolen by other players
  • Alliance ranking with 7 reward categories

💰 Recommended Passes:

🏆 Season Pass

$25 USD

Benefits for the full 80 days. Includes additional rewards and progression bonuses.

📅 Weekly Pass

$10 USD

Weekly benefits. Purchased multiple times during the season grants cumulative bonuses.

🌵 Resource Plot System

Plots are the central mechanic of Season 2. You start with 10 plots available and the number increases over time.

🌵 Desert Heart (Cactus) — Levels 1 to 14

Function: Main resource. Required to upgrade most S2 buildings.

Production: The higher the cactus level, the more it produces per hour.

Recommended distribution: 70% of your plots.

💎 Blue Crystal — Levels 1 to 14

Function: Secondary resource equally needed for certain S2 buildings.

Production: The higher the crystal level, the more it produces per hour.

Recommended distribution: 30% of your plots.

🌵 Cactus 70%
💎 Crystal 30%
⚠️ IMPORTANT: The 70/30 distribution may vary depending on which buildings you still need to upgrade. Adjust according to your current needs.

⚔️ How to Conquer Plots

  • Attack the plot with your troops — requires strong formations and virus resistance based on the plot level.
  • After winning, it takes ~10 minutes to take control. Your troops can return once done.
  • The higher the plot level, the more virus resistance you need.
  • Other players can steal your plots — stay alert and defend them.

📊 Plot Strategy

  • ⚡ Capture plots as soon as possible — they run out fast, especially high-level ones.
  • 🎯 Prioritize high-level plots — they produce significantly more resources.
  • ⚠️ As the season progresses, high-level crystals and cactus become the scarcest and most contested.
  • 🏰 Coordinate with your alliance to defend key plots.

🧪 Virus Institute TOP PRIORITY

Function: Produces antigens and antibodies to increase your virus resistance. Higher resistance lets you occupy higher-level plots.

Quantity: Up to 4 Institutes if your alliance has all 3 Alliance Centers built.

Strategy: The MOST IMPORTANT building — key to accessing high-level plots.

🏛️ Land Management Office

Function: Level up → more space and available plots.

When to upgrade: Second priority after the Virus Institute.

⚔️ Military Fortress

Function: Raises morale → greatly increases attack capacity.

Extra: With Combat specialization level 25 you get an additional +1 Military Fortress.

🛡️ Defense Training Center

Function: Improves your troops' defense capacity.

🎯 Strategic Barracks

Function: Improves individual combat and provides extreme power to your formations.

🏰 Alliance Center x3

Function: Required for Glory War and to unlock dependent buildings. Any alliance member can build them.

⚠️ REMEMBER: The higher the building level, the more plot resources are required. Plan your production ahead of time.

🎯 Recommended Upgrade Order:

  1. Virus Institute — Always top priority.
  2. Land Management Office — To get more plots.
  3. Alliance Center x3 — Coordinate with your alliance.
  4. Military Fortress, Strategic Barracks and Defense Center — No specific order, based on available resources.

🎓 Specialization Room

Choose your specialization path from level 1 to 30, with skills adapted to the desert environment.

🔀 Choice System:

  • Level <30: You will be SUPPORT by default.
  • Level 30: You can choose between SUPPORT or COMBAT.

🤝 Specialization: SUPPORT

Recommended for: Most players. Maximizes resource production and individual progress.

🌵 Oasis Production Boost (Lv.1)

Desert Heart production +3% per level.

Strategy: High priority — more cactus = more resources for buildings.

💎 Crystal Refinement (Lv.10)

Blue Crystal production +3% per level.

Strategy: Complements the cactus boost to balance resources.

🤝 United as One (Lv.20)

Alliance help effect +10%.

Strategy: Useful to speed up allied constructions and research.

🦠 Resistance Reinforcement (Lv.20)

The Virus Institute grants +1 virus resistance per level.

Strategy: Essential — more resistance = access to higher-level plots.

🏗️ Cooperative Construction (Lv.15 — Max Level 2)

You and an ally both get +15% construction speed for 1 hour. Cooldown: 24h.

Strategy: Use it when both of you are upgrading important buildings.

⚔️ Specialization: COMBAT

Recommended for: Level 30 players with high hero power. Maximizes combat capability to conquer and defend plots.

🪖 Urgent Recruitment

Instantly recruits soldiers. Cooldown: once per day.

Strategy: Useful to replenish troops after plot battles.

🎭 Disfraz

El enemigo no obtendrá información al explorarte durante 1 hora.

Strategy: Useful before attacking enemy plots to maintain the element of surprise.

⚡ Quick Attack

Your focus attack launches faster. Limit: 5 times per week.

Strategy: Ideal for Glory War and for conquering plots before the enemy.

🔄 Immediate Return

Your troops return to base immediately. Limit: 2 times per day.

Strategy: To quickly reposition troops between plots or escape from attacks.

📚 How to Obtain Experience Books:

  1. 🏆 Specialization Pass ($20): The fastest method.
  2. 🏗️ Upgrade season buildings: Each upgrade grants books.
  3. 🏙️ Conquer and defend cities: Provide constant rewards.
  4. ⚔️ Attack enemy headquarters: During Alliance War.

⚔️ Glory War & Ranking

The most important event of the season. The winner can steal season points from the loser.

Season Points = Personal Glory (all members) + City Points

How to Earn Points:

  • Personal Glory: Actively participating in battles and season events.
  • Cities: Occupying and holding cities generates passive points. Accumulated points are NOT lost when you lose the city.
  • Glory War: Winning allows you to steal points from the enemy.

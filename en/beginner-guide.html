📚 Beginner's Manual

Complete guide to start on the right foot in Last Z Survival Shooter. Proven strategies, perfect timing and mistakes to avoid.

🎮 Initial Tutorial (First Hours)

What to Expect When Starting?

When starting Last Z, the game will guide you through a basic tutorial where you'll learn the fundamental mechanics:

  • Basic building construction - You'll learn to build and upgrade
  • Growth Tasks - Missions that give you resources and rewards
  • Combat against zombies - Swipe left/right to move heroes in battle
  • Resource management - Food, Wood, Z-Coin, Electricity

Your First Heroes

When you start the game, you automatically receive two heroes:

Angelina RANK B

Quality: Basic starting hero

Recommended use: Complete initial missions and zombie combat in early stages. Not a high-level hero, but functional to start.

William RANK B

Quality: Basic starting hero

Recommended use: Similar to Angelina, useful for early stages but not a long-term investment. Use it temporarily until you get better heroes.

⚠️ Important - Don't invest too much in these heroes

Angelina and William are rank B heroes (the lowest quality). The game has rank A and S heroes that are much better. Don't spend valuable resources upgrading Angelina or William - just use them to complete the tutorial and early stages.

Stage System

The game has a stage system you unlock by advancing on the map killing zombies. Some key stages:

  • Stage 5: Special offer appears for Sophia for $1 (BUY IT!)
  • Stage 7: You unlock the Club (hero recruitment)
  • Stage 14: You receive Katrina for free (rank A hero)

💎 Must-Have Offer: Sophia for $1 (Stage 45)

🏰 Key Buildings & Priority Order

Main Building: HQ

The Main Headquarters (also called simply "HQ") is the most important building in the game. Your HQ level determines the maximum level all other buildings can reach.

💡 Golden Rule

Always prioritize upgrading your HQ to the highest possible level. All other buildings are limited by your HQ level.

Resource Production Buildings

These buildings generate resources automatically:

  • Farm: Produces Food (MOST important resource - troops consume it)
  • Lumber Mill: Produces Wood (essential for construction)
  • Residence: Produces Z-Coin (also from events/missions)
  • Generator: Produces Electricity (less critical - usually surplus)

Military Buildings

To recruit and manage troops:

Recruitment Camps

  • Shooting Range: Recruits sniper-type soldiers
  • Bikers Camp: Recruits motorized units
  • Raiders Camp: Recruits assault units

Important: The higher the camp level, the better quality troops you can recruit.

Military Center

Function: Manages your troops and determines the maximum soldier capacity you can have.

Military Center level = total troop capacity. Upgrade it constantly.

Other Important Buildings

  • Alliance Center: Unlocks at HQ level 5. Lets you join alliances, get help with construction/research, and receive reinforcements when attacked.
  • Laboratory: Unlocks at HQ level 7. Here you research technologies that grant permanent buffs.
  • Club: Unlocks at Stage 7. Recruit new heroes with tickets.
  • Hospital: Heals wounded troops (costs resources but avoids losing them)

⚠️ About the Warehouse

Don't prioritize upgrading the Warehouse unless it's a requirement to upgrade your HQ. Reason: although it protects resources from looting, if attacked you'll still have wounded troops in the Hospital that cost resources to heal. It's better to spend your resources on productive upgrades than to stockpile them.

Recommended Priority Order

  1. Main HQ - Always at maximum possible
  2. Troop Camps - To have a strong army
  3. Military Center - Increases troop capacity
  4. Farm and Lumber Mill - Critical resource production
  5. Laboratory - Technologies give permanent advantage
  6. Alliance Center - Help and reinforcements
  7. Required buildings - Whatever HQ needs to upgrade

⚔️ Formation & Combat System

What Are Formations?

Formations are your combat teams. Each formation can have:

  • 5 heroes (leaders that provide special skills)
  • Troops (soldiers that fight under the heroes' command)

Progressive Unlock

Formations are unlocked as you level up:

  • Formation 1: Available from the start
  • Formation 2: Unlocks at mid levels
  • Formation 3: Unlocks at approximately HQ level 14

💡 Formation Strategy

Formation 1: Your strongest team. Use it for PvP combat and hard zombies.

Formations 2 and 3: Secondary teams for resource farming, easier missions, or as reinforcement.

Invest primarily in Formation 1 heroes - don't spread resources upgrading 15 heroes at once.

🦸 Hero Guide for Beginners

Rank System

Heroes are classified into 3 quality ranks:

  • RANK S - Best heroes in the game (very hard to get)
  • RANK A - Good heroes (accessible and viable)
  • RANK B - Basic heroes (for starting only)

Heroes You Get for Free

Katrina RANK A

How to get her: Free upon reaching Stage 14

Quality: Good hero for beginners

S Version: Later you can unlock her S version by killing zombies in special missions

Recommendation: Worth investing in - she's a solid hero that will serve you long-term

Fiona RANK A

How to get her: Club recruitment (Stage 7+) with tickets

Quality: Excellent for beginners

Recommendation: If you get her, she's a great option for Formation 1. Invest in her.

Club - Hero Recruitment

Upon reaching Stage 7, you unlock the Club where you can recruit heroes using special tickets.

💡 Smart Ticket Usage

Save the recruitment tickets you get in early stages. When you unlock the Club, use them all at once for a chance at good heroes.

EXP investment priority:

  1. Rank S heroes (if you have any)
  2. Rank A heroes (Katrina, Fiona, etc.)
  3. DO NOT spend EXP on rank B heroes

🤝 Alliances - Why They Are Crucial

From HQ level 5, you can build the Alliance Center and join an alliance.

✅ Join a STRONG Alliance as soon as possible

One of the most important decisions in Last Z is choosing a good alliance. Don't stay in weak or inactive alliances - look for top alliances on your server.

Benefits of Being in an Alliance

  • Construction/Research Help: Members can help you reduce wait times
  • Reinforcements: If someone attacks you, your alliance can defend you
  • Alliance Technologies: Shared permanent buffs (researched in the Laboratory)
  • Alliance Events: Group rewards much better than solo
  • Donations: Resource exchange with other members
  • Alliance Versus: Weekly event with great rewards (see dedicated section below)

How to Choose a Good Alliance

  • Minimum 20-30 ACTIVE daily members
  • High alliance technology level
  • Active participation in events
  • Chat communication (Discord recommended)
  • Controlled territories on the map

⚠️ Common Mistake

Don't stay in weak alliances out of loyalty. If your alliance isn't active or doesn't participate in events, find a better one. Changing alliances is normal and necessary to progress.

🎯 Key Events: Full Preparation

Full Preparation is one of the most important events in the game. It runs in 4-hour cycles with different phases.

How Does It Work?

Every 4 hours, the event changes phase. In each phase, certain actions give multiplied points:

🦸 Heroes Phase

Spend hero EXP

Upgrade skills

Level up heroes

🧟 Zombies Phase

Kill zombies on map

Complete rallies

Clear stages

🏗️ Construction Phase

Upgrade buildings

Use speed-ups

Complete constructions

⚔️ Troops Phase

Recruit soldiers

Train army

Upgrade camps

🔬 Research Phase

Research technologies

Use speed-ups

Complete studies

💡 Full Preparation Strategy

DON'T do everything immediately - wait for the right phase:

  • Accumulate hero EXP → Spend it in Heroes Phase
  • Save construction speed-ups → Use them in Construction Phase
  • Plan researches → Start them in Research Phase
  • Recruit troops in bulk → Troops Phase

This way you multiply your points and get better event rewards.

⏰ Timing Optimization

Before logging off at night:

  • Start long constructions/researches (6-8 hours)
  • Activate resource production buffs
  • Leave troops gathering on the map
  • If Construction Phase is active, use speed-ups before sleeping

Take advantage of the hours you're NOT playing for passive progress.

🏆 Alliance Versus (Weekly Event)

After 14 days of playing, every Monday the Alliance Versus begins, a competitive event lasting a full week.

Weekly Calendar

Monday: Vehicles 🚗

Objective: Upgrade vehicles using blueprints and keys

Bonus points: Each vehicle upgrade adds points for your alliance

Tip: Save blueprints and keys from the previous week to use on this day

Tuesday: Refugees 🏕️

Objective: Build shelters and recruit refugees

Bonus points: Building and recruiting refugees

Tip: The "Butler" and "Diplomat" refugees are the most valuable

Wednesday: Research 🔬

Objective: Research technologies in the Laboratory

Bonus points: Starting and completing researches

Tip: Save pending researches for this day

Thursday: Heroes 🦸

Objective: Upgrade heroes, skills and armor

Bonus points: Level up, upgrade skills, equip armor

Tip: Combine with Full Preparation if Heroes Phase is active

Friday: Troops ⚔️

Objective: Recruit soldiers in bulk

Bonus points: Amount of troops trained

Tip: Save resources all week to recruit heavily on this day

Saturday: Combat 💥

Objective: Kill enemy soldiers

Bonus points: Attacking rival alliance = more points

Tip: This day is all-out war - coordinate with your alliance

✅ Resource Management Strategy

Plan ahead:

  • Save resources days before the Versus
  • Don't spend everything on Monday - spread it across the week
  • If you can align Versus with Full Preparation = double benefit
  • Communicate with your alliance about weekly strategy

💪 Important Goal: 8 Million Power

One of the first important goals is reaching 8 million overall power.

🏆 Reward: Combat Fortress Skin

Upon reaching 8M power, you receive a premium skin for your HQ with excellent buffs:

  • Troop ATK +8.0%
  • Hero EXP Buff +10.0%
  • Z-Coin Bonus +10.0%

How to Increase Power Quickly?

  1. Recruit troops - Gives the most power (thousands per recruitment)
  2. Upgrade buildings - Each level adds significant power
  3. Level up heroes - Invest EXP in your best heroes
  4. Research technologies - Permanent power
  5. Complete missions - Give resources for all of the above

💡 Efficient Focus

To reach 8M quickly:

  • Upgrade your Troop Camps to recruit higher-level soldiers
  • Upgrade the Military Center for more capacity
  • Recruit constantly - troops are massive power
  • Don't waste resources on decorative buildings

🚫 Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ The Biggest Beginner Mistakes

1. Attacking HQs stronger than you

ALWAYS scout before attacking. If the HQ has more power than you, you'll lose troops without gaining anything. Only attack weaker HQs to loot resources efficiently.

2. Staying in weak alliances too long

Loyalty is fine, but if your alliance isn't active, it's holding you back. Look for top alliances to grow faster.

3. Getting frustrated because others spend money

There will be stronger P2W (pay to win) players. Don't compare yourself - focus on your own progress. The game is fully playable without spending (or spending little).

4. Spending diamonds on the wrong things

DON'T spend diamonds on:

  • Time speed-ups (use the free ones from events)
  • Basic resources (farm on the map)
  • Random chests (bad investment)

DO spend diamonds on:

  • VIP level (best buffs in the game)
  • Second builder (if you didn't buy it with $)
  • Events with guaranteed rewards

5. Investing in rank B heroes

Don't waste resources upgrading Angelina, William or other B heroes. Wait to get A or S heroes before investing heavily.

6. Not using free speed-ups

Event speed-ups MUST be used, especially in Full Preparation. Don't stockpile them indefinitely - use them strategically.

7. Over-upgrading the Warehouse

Unless it's required to upgrade HQ, don't prioritize the Warehouse. It's better to spend resources on productive upgrades.

8. Not participating in events

Events like Full Preparation and Alliance Versus give enormous rewards. Active participation = progressing 2-3x faster.

⚡ Optimization & Timing Tips

Maximize Your Time (Even When Not Playing)

🌙 Before Sleeping (Night Routine)

  • Start long constructions (6-8 hours) so they finish while you sleep
  • Activate production buffs (+25% or +50%) - maximize while offline
  • Send formations to gather on 6-8 hour resource tiles
  • Start researches that take all night
  • Recruit troops in bulk if you have resources
  • If there's an active event, plan what to do when you wake up

☀️ When Waking Up (Morning Routine)

  • Collect resource production from all buildings
  • Check troops that returned from farming - send again
  • Complete daily missions for rewards
  • Check active events (Full Preparation, Alliance Versus)
  • Help alliance members with constructions

Smart Resource Management

💰 When to Spend vs When to Save

SPEND resources when:

  • There's a phase/event that multiplies points (Full Preparation)
  • It's a specific Alliance Versus day
  • You need to meet requirements to upgrade HQ
  • You have an active shield and are near the storage limit

SAVE resources when:

  • Alliance Versus is coming up (especially troop Friday)
  • You want to make a big HQ upgrade
  • There's an upcoming event with spending rewards
  • You don't have a shield and are stockpiling too much (you'll get attacked)

PvP & Looting

⚔️ Attack Strategy

When to start attacking: Day 3-4, or when you reach HQ level 10+

Correct process:

  1. Scout: ALWAYS scout first (costs nothing)
  2. Check resources: Make sure they have good Food and/or Wood
  3. Check power: Only attack HQs WEAKER than you
  4. Attack: Send your Formation 1 (the strongest)
  5. Return: Enable "Auto-return" so troops come back automatically

Resources you can loot:

  • ✅ Food (main resource to farm)
  • ✅ Wood (also very useful)
  • ❌ Z-Coin (requires special license - ignore at start)
  • ⚡ Electricity (usually surplus, don't prioritize)

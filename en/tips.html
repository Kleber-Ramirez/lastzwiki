TIP 01

Best Refugees to Recruit

🦸 Refugees

The Butler and Diplomat refugees are the most valuable in the game. Prioritize recruiting them to massively accelerate your progression.

🎯 The 2 Most Important Refugees

Refugees grant permanent passive abilities. Not all are equally useful — these 2 are the ones you must prioritize:

🏗️ #1 — Butler

Main Ability: Construction Speed

Bonus: Free acceleration time when building

  • Drastically reduces building construction time
  • Grants additional free acceleration time
  • Useful from level 1 to endgame
  • Saves days or weeks of accumulated waiting

🤝 #2 — Diplomat

Main Ability: Alliance Help Limit

Bonus: More time reduced per help received

  • Increases how many alliance helps you can receive
  • Combines perfectly with the Butler
  • In active alliances, complete buildings almost instantly

🌟 Rarities

Even a green Butler beats other higher rarity refugees. Prioritize the type over rarity.

🎯 Recruitment Strategy

Priority 1: Butler → Priority 2: Diplomat → Priority 3: upgrade rarity.

💎 F2P Tip: Worth spending diamonds on these 2. They are one of the best investments in the game.

🏢 Recruitment Costs

  • 1st Refugee: 500 coupons or diamonds
  • 2nd Refugee: 2,000 coupons or diamonds
  • 3rd Refugee: 5,000 coupons or diamonds

Available refugees change every 12 hours. You get a free refresh after each recruitment.

TIP 02

Recycle your Blue Skill Manuals

🦸 Heroes

Leftover blue manuals can be recycled into boxes containing diamonds or Rank S hero fragments. Don't let them pile up unused.

📦 The 3 Manual Ranks

  • 🔵 Blue Manual → For Rank B heroes
  • 🟣 Purple Manual → For Rank A heroes
  • 🟠 Orange Manual → For Rank S heroes

✅ How to Recycle

Step 1: go to Warehouse → Hero tab.

Step 2: find leftover Blue Manuals.

Step 3: if all your Rank B heroes are maxed, you'll see "Recycle".

Step 4: each manual becomes a Random Box that can give: Resources, 💎 Diamonds or ⭐ Rank S Fragments.

💎 Key: open boxes in batches of 1,000. More at once = higher probability of Diamonds or S Fragments.

💡 Tip contributed by @Daff21 — thanks to the community!

TIP 03

Boost Your Formation Attack

⚔️ Combat

Align 5 heroes of the same faction to get ATQ +115%. Prioritize Blood Rose in your first formation and its support buildings.

There are 3 factions: 🔴 Blood Rose, 🔵 Wings of Dawn and 🟡 Guardian. With 5 heroes of the same faction you get ATQ +115%.

🎯 Priority Order

1st — Blood Rose: easiest to get at the start + Toxic Mist Garden building.

2nd — Wings of Dawn: boosted by Dawn Tower.

3rd — Guardian: boosted by Steel Factory.

🏗️ Faction Buildings

1. Toxic Mist Garden → HIGH Priority

2. Dawn Tower → MEDIUM Priority

3. Steel Factory → MEDIUM Priority

💡 Rules

✅ Keep formations pure (5 heroes same faction).

❌ Don't mix factions — you lose the +115% buff.

❌ Don't upgrade all 3 buildings at the same time.

💡 F2P: The +115% buff makes a huge difference in PvP and events. No money needed, just strategy.

TIP 04

Armor Rotation in Caravan

⚔️ Strategy

Transfer your best armor between formations to maximize rounds. Move equipment from Rose → Dawn → Guardian as you progress.

🏛️ The 3 Caravan Fields

  • 🔴 Blood Rose Field
  • 🔵 Wings of Dawn Field
  • 🟡 Guardian Order Field

⚙️ Rotation Steps

Step 1: enter Rose field with your 1st formation and best armor. Advance as far as possible and exit.

Step 2: equip that armor on your 2nd formation (Dawn). Enter and advance.

Step 3: move armor to your 3rd formation (Guardian). Enter and advance.

Step 4: return armor to your 1st formation when done.

💡 Why It Works

  • Your 2nd and 3rd formations are normally weaker
  • Borrowed armor = more rounds = better rewards
  • 100% F2P — costs no resources

⚠️ Remember: always restore armor to your 1st formation after Caravan ends.

TIP 05

Enable Auto Return for Troops

⚔️ Combat

Always check the "Auto Return" checkbox before marching. Your troops will come back automatically after combat — no manual searching needed.

✅ How to Enable It

Step 1: click on the target → select "Attack".

Step 2: find the "Auto Return" checkbox in the panel.

Step 3: make sure it's checked ✓ green.

Step 4: click "March". Troops return automatically after combat.

⚔️ Always verify: don't assume it's enabled by default. Check before every attack, especially when farming multiple targets in a row.

TIP 06

Fast Reinforcement in Your Territory

⚔️ Mobility

Inside alliance territory, reinforcement is 3× faster than normal movement. Use it to reach treasures, strategic points and support allies urgently.

💡 How to Use It

  1. Identify an allied base or city near the target
  2. Send a reinforcement to that intermediate base (3× speed)
  3. From there, launch toward the final target

⚠️ Restriction

The 3× speed only works inside your alliance territory. Outside, normal speed applies.

🎯 When to Use It

  • To arrive first at alliance treasures
  • In alliance events where speed is critical
  • To quickly support rally points

⚡ In alliance events, seconds determine who gets the loot. Mastering reinforcement gives you a real edge.

TIP 08

Efficient Healing with Alliance Help

🤝 Alliance

Don't use "Heal All" at once. Healing in small batches lets your alliance reduce the time and heal your troops without spending speedups.

❌ The Common Mistake

Selecting "Heal All" at once → very long time → you spend speedups.

✅ The Right Strategy

Step 1: DON'T use "Heal All".

Step 2: check how many alliance members are online.

Step 3: heal a small batch — the time will be short.

Step 4: your alliance reduces that time with their helps.

Step 5: repeat until all troops are healed without spending anything.

🎯 Best moment: after events like "The Tyrant", when many members are online. Heal all your troops without spending anything.

TIP 09

Quick Donation in Alliance Technologies

🤝 Alliance

Hold down the donate button instead of individual clicks. Activates automatic multipliers ×2, ×5, ×10 to donate much faster.

✅ The Trick

Hold down the donate button. Multipliers will appear:

  • 🔥 ×2 → donate double
  • 🔥 ×5 → donate 5 times more
  • 🔥 ×10 → donate 10 times more

🔥 Extra: contribution points are used in the Alliance Shop for speedups, resources and fragments. The more you donate, the more you can buy!

TIP 10

Locate Bases with Scout Reports

🤝 Server

Click on the coordinates in the scout report and the map takes you directly to that base. No manual searching — essential for coordinating alliance attacks.

✅ The Trick

Step 1: open the report (from mail or shared chat).

Step 2: below the player name you'll see coordinates (e.g.: X:324 Y:772).

Step 3: click directly on those coordinates.

Result: the map automatically goes to that base. No manual search.

💡 Use Cases

  • Coordinate attacks — everyone reaches the target quickly
  • Check distance before deciding to attack
  • Find weak bases shared in chat

🔍 Works on any scout report — mail or chat. Use it whenever you need to locate a base quickly.

TIP 11

Save Resource Boxes for High Level

💰 Economy

Golden boxes scale with your HQ level. The same golden box at HQ level 27 gives 10x more resources than at level 15. Don't open them early.

📦 Box Types

  • 🟦 Blue → Use freely when needed

  • 🟨 Golden → DON'T open until level 27+

TIP 12

Create Secondary Farm Accounts

💰 Economy

Secondary accounts to generate extra resources and send them to your main account. Two methods: Dual Space or free teleport during events.

📱 Method 1: Dual Space

  1. Download Dual Space (Android/iOS)
  2. Install a second copy of the game inside it
  3. Go to Profile → Account → Character Management → Create New
  4. Select your server

🚀 Method 2: Free TP (Saturdays)

  1. During Server War, teleport to another zone
  2. Go to Profile → Account → Character Management → Create New
  3. Select your original server

💡 Efficient Farm Rules

  • Join your own alliance for direct donations
  • Only upgrade production — keep power low
  • Use cheap shields for protection

⚠️ Check game rules on multi-accounts. Avoid using them to manipulate rankings.

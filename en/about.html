👋 About Last Z Wiki

"From a personal project to a tool for the whole community"

🎯 Our Mission

Last Z Wiki was created to fill a gap in the Last Z Survival Shooter community: clear, up-to-date and easy-to-use information.

Our goal: To make Last Z Wiki the go-to reference resource for new and veteran players, providing interactive guides, updated strategies and a platform where the community can learn and grow together.

📖 Project Origins

Founded by Hypnos, a web developer and veteran Last Z player, this wiki grew from a real need observed in Alliance SHK.

After 8 months of experience across multiple servers, including a period as R5 (alliance leader), it became clear that the community needed a centralized, well-designed resource.

September 2025

Development begins. A small project to help alliance SHK with their recurring questions.

October - January 2026

The project grows. From a personal hobby to a complete wiki with professional design and interactive content.

February 2026

Official public launch of Last Z Wiki for the entire global community.

What started as a tool for my alliance became a passion: helping the entire Last Z community.

✨ Why Is Last Z Wiki Different?

🎨 Professional Interface

Developed by a web development professional, prioritizing modern UI/UX and intuitive navigation.

⚡ Interactive Content

Dynamic, visual guides — not boring static documents. Information that adapts to your needs.

🎮 Real Experience

Lysia

Tier S 🌹 Blood Rose P2W
Max Level: Sede × 5 (Sede 30 = Lv.150) Season: S3 Role: Combate — Capacidad de tropas Position: Back row Ideal Troop: Assaulters How to get: Hero Pass (seasonal)

⚠️ Important note about Skill 3: Lysia's skill 3 is 'Active in Expeditions'. This means it only works when Lysia is deployed in your active formation. It's different from other heroes whose skill 3 is 'Effective for all troops' and applies even without the hero being in the active team.

💡 💡 My personal tip: Su habilidad 2 escala mucho con estrellas — priorízala. Úsala con Bella para activar su bonus de habilidad 4 y maximizar resistencia al daño de tus Asaltantes.

⭐ Is this hero worth it?

Yes, Lysia is worth it if you play Blood Rose. As a Tier S hero with a combate — capacidad de tropas role, she's a core part of the faction. Remember: focus on the 3 strongest S heroes of your faction — don't spread fragments equally across all 5.

🕐 When to use this hero?

Use her as soon as you get her. Her skill 3 only applies when deployed — Prioritize her level and skill 3 first. She works best in back row with Assaulters troops.

✅ Advantages

  • Tier S hero — direct impact on your march power
  • Role: Combate — Capacidad de tropas
  • Synergy with same-faction heroes

❌ Disadvantages

  • Only available with season pass or special events
  • Requires fragment investment to maximize
  • Skill 3 'Active in Expeditions' — only applies when deployed

🏆 Investment Priority

I focus my fragments and resources on the 3 strongest S heroes of my faction. The 2 weakest will be replaced as I advance through seasons — there's no point investing heavily in them. Season faction order: S1 Blood Rose → S2 Wings of Dawn → S3 Guard of Order.

⚔️ Formation Position

Back row — Assaulters. Align 5 Blood Rose heroes to activate the maximum faction bonus (+10% troop HP).

📅 Best Season

S3 — Combate — Capacidad de tropas.

💎 How to get S hero fragments

You can get S hero fragments by upgrading your VIP level, completing daily missions (looting trucks, sending trucks), participating in popular seasonal events, and at the end of each season where your alliance leader distributes rewards based on ranking — including diamonds and exclusive season tickets to recruit heroes you didn't buy. That's why being in a strong, active alliance is essential.

Giselle

Tier B 🌹 Blood Rose F2P
Max Level: Sede × 5 (Sede 30 = Lv.150) Season: comenzado 1 Role: Recolección — Madera Position: Secondary formations Ideal Troop: Assaulters How to get: Recruiting heroes at the Club

💡 💡 My personal tip: Héroe de recolección pura — su valor está en la habilidad 3 de madera. No la uses en combate, ponla en formaciones secundarias para farmeo de recursos.

⭐ Is this hero worth it?

Giselle is a Tier B hero — useful early game and in secondary formations. Don't invest heavy resources here while your S hero team isn't complete. Once it is, Giselle becomes support in your formations 2 or 3.

🕐 When to use this hero?

Use her in the first weeks of the game or in secondary formations once your main faction already has 5 S heroes. Universal A hero fragments you earn in-game are perfect for this hero.

✅ Advantages

  • Free — accessible to all players
  • Role: Recolección — Madera
  • Useful in secondary formations

❌ Disadvantages

  • Gets replaced when you get Blood Rose S heroes
  • Doesn't scale well in advanced seasons

🏆 Investment Priority

Don't invest EXP in this hero while your S heroes aren't maxed. Save your EXP to immediately level your S heroes when you unlock the next HQ level. Always prioritize heroes in your 3 main formations first. Universal A hero fragments you receive in the game are great for this hero in secondary formations.

⚔️ Formation Position

Secondary formations — Assaulters. Align 5 Blood Rose heroes to activate the maximum faction bonus (+10% troop HP).

📅 Best Season

comenzado 1 — Recolección — Madera.

💎 How to get S hero fragments

You can get S hero fragments by upgrading your VIP level, completing daily missions (looting trucks, sending trucks), participating in popular seasonal events, and at the end of each season where your alliance leader distributes rewards based on ranking — including diamonds and exclusive season tickets to recruit heroes you didn't buy. That's why being in a strong, active alliance is essential.

♻️ Tip: Recycle your Blue Manuals

Over time, all your Rank B heroes will have all 4 skills maxed. From that point, Blue Manuals accumulate with no use. The solution: recycle them in your Storage → Hero tab → find Blue Skill Manuals and select 'Recycle'. Each blue manual becomes a Random Basic Resource Box that can give: wood, electricity, food, Z Coins, 💎 diamonds or even ⭐ Rank S hero fragments.

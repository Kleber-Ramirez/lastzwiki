Evelyn

Tier S 🛡️ Guard of Order P2W
Max Level: Sede × 5 (Sede 30 = Lv.150) Season: s1 Role: Soporte — Entrenamiento de Motoristas Position: Back row Ideal Troop: Riders How to get: Hero Pass (seasonal)

💡 💡 My personal tip: Héroe S de temporada 1 de Guardia del Orden con buena pasiva de entrenamiento de Motoristas. Su equipo exclusivo disponible la hace competitiva.

⭐ Is this hero worth it?

Yes, Evelyn is worth it if you play Guard of Order. As a Tier S hero with a soporte — entrenamiento de motoristas role, she's a core part of the faction. Remember: focus on the 3 strongest S heroes of your faction — don't spread fragments equally across all 5.

🕐 When to use this hero?

Use her as soon as you get her. Prioritize her level and skill 3 first. She works best in back row with Riders troops.

✅ Advantages

  • Tier S hero — direct impact on your march power
  • Role: Soporte — Entrenamiento de Motoristas
  • Synergy with same-faction heroes

❌ Disadvantages

  • Only available with season pass or special events
  • Requires fragment investment to maximize

🏆 Investment Priority

I focus my fragments and resources on the 3 strongest S heroes of my faction. The 2 weakest will be replaced as I advance through seasons — there's no point investing heavily in them. Season faction order: S1 Blood Rose → S2 Wings of Dawn → S3 Guard of Order.

⚔️ Formation Position

Back row — Riders. Align 5 Guard of Order heroes to activate the maximum faction bonus (+10% troop HP).

📅 Best Season

s1 — Soporte — Entrenamiento de Motoristas.

💎 How to get S hero fragments

You can get S hero fragments by upgrading your VIP level, completing daily missions (looting trucks, sending trucks), participating in popular seasonal events, and at the end of each season where your alliance leader distributes rewards based on ranking — including diamonds and exclusive season tickets to recruit heroes you didn't buy. That's why being in a strong, active alliance is essential.

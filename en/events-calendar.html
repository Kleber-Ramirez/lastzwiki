Event Calendar

Complete guide to the weekly Hot Events cycle, exclusive season events and special events on key dates. Identify which event is running on your server and apply the right strategy to maximize your rewards.

🔥 Hot Events

3-week cycle that rotates continuously across all servers. Each server may be on a different week of the cycle. Identify which one is running on yours and prepare in advance.

💡

How to know which week your server is on? Open the game → Events section → find which of the 3 Hot Events is active. Then come back here and apply the matching strategy.

Hypnos' Tip — Play the long game

Something many players don't know: Hot Event items carry over between cycles. You're not forced to spend them every week the event appears. If you save patiently over several cycles, you can aim for higher-value rewards when the event comes back to your server.

🎰
Gacha Go

Save your grey keys between cycles. The more you accumulate, the more chests you can open at once when the event returns — and more chances at rare rewards.

🎯
Shooting Loot

Bullets don't disappear when the event ends. Save them week after week and enter the next cycle with enough ammo to go straight to Round 12 or beyond.

🃏
Lucky Discounter

Accumulate coupons patiently. You don't need to spend them every cycle — wait until you have enough to go straight for the guaranteed 90% draws and get the most out of them.

The key isn't to play more — it's to play smart. A patient F2P player can get the same rewards as a spender, they just need planning.

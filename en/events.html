Event: Canyon Battle (Canyon Clash)

📅 Cronograma del evento
Monday & Tuesday – Voting: Alliance members vote on the event start time. Although everyone can vote, the final time is decided by the leader or an R4.
Wednesday – Registration: The R4 or R5 registers up to 20 main players and 30 substitutes. Two commanders are also chosen.
Thursday – Matchmaking: The system matches alliances based on the maximum power of selected players.
Friday – Battle Day: The war begins. Main players can enter 5 min before start; substitutes enter after the event starts. Total duration is 45 minutes.

Event Objective Occupy strategic buildings and zones to accumulate alliance points. The alliance with the most points at the end wins, teleporting the energy gives you 100k pts, Teleport is free every 3 minutes.
The 2 commanders can mark points on the map to organize strategy and direct the 4 alliance groups toward specific objectives.

Battle for the Capital

🏰 Main Rules

1. When the Capital opens, any alliance can send troops to conquer it.
2. Players earn competition points by fighting or defending the Capital.
3. If an alliance reaches 100% occupation, it gains full control. If time runs out first, the alliance with the most occupation progress wins.
4. The winning alliance leader can appoint the President of the war zone.
5. The President gets special privileges: assign roles, distribute packages and issue decrees.
6. The winning alliance receives exclusive power upgrades and bonuses.
7. In the first edition of the event, players can also earn point rewards based on their performance.

Zombie Tyrant

1. The alliance must have more than 20 members to participate.
2. The event must be scheduled manually by an R4 or R5. Once scheduled, the Tyrant will remain active for 15 mins.
3. Members must launch rally attacks to deal damage to the boss. 4. When combat ends, rewards are distributed based on:

  • The total alliance damage (collective reward).
  • The individual damage of each member (personal reward).

5. The higher the total damage of the alliance, the better the rewards for all participants.
6. Players with the highest damage will earn better rewards, and the MVP member can multiply alliance rewards between 1 and 10 times based on total damage.

Tips

• Gather the most active members before the Tyrant.
• Make sure everyone has their troops ready before the event.
• Coordinate the use of buffs and high-damage heroes for better damage.
• Remember to move the rally point to a central area to reposition the Tyrant!

Server War

🏆 What is it?

PvP event where servers compete 1v1 to conquer the enemy capital. Direct elimination system until a champion is crowned.

⚔️ How It Works

Phase 1: Point Accumulation (Sunday - Friday)
Earn points by completing daily activities: Alliance War, Full Preparation, Arena, Truck Raid and Zombie Boss.
Result: More points = Attacker | Fewer points = Defender.

Phase 2: Battle for the Capital (Saturday)
The Attacker strikes the enemy capital (goal: 100% occupation).
The Defender protects their capital by blocking TP zones.
Victory: Conquer 100% or have higher progress when time ends.

💡 Tips

• Coordinate mass attacks with your alliance.
- Establish strategic bases near the capital.
- Server unity is key to winning.

Alliance Versus

📅 How It Works

Duration: Monday to Saturday (6 days).
Requirement: Alliance with 20+ members.

Daily Themes:

  • Day 1 (Monday): Modified Vehicle Upgrade - 1 pt
  • Day 2 (Tuesday): Build Shelter - 2 pts
  • Day 3 (Wednesday): Research Technologies - 2 pts
  • Day 4 (Thursday): Train Heroes - 2 pts
  • Day 5 (Friday): Overall Progress / Recruit Troops - 2 pts
  • Day 6 (Saturday): Defeat Enemies - 4 pts

Victory: The alliance with the most total points at the end of Saturday wins the duel.
MVP: The player with the most personal points of the day gets special rewards.

🗡️ Saturday – War Zone Duel

On the last day you can teleport to the enemy server and attack rival bases to earn extra points. Free TP between servers during the event.

💡 Tips

• If you're winning, use 24h shields to protect yourself from attacks.
- Loot in groups (minimum 3 players) for better safety.
- Save resources if facing weak alliances; keep them for harder weeks.
- Reinforce alliance members under attack.
- If you've already lost, arrange a "friendly tank vs tank" on Saturday to get rewards.

Server Migration

🧭 What is Migration?

Event that allows players to change servers at the end of season 2 to join more competitive states or ones with better alliances. It happens after season 2 ends, before the next season begins.
Duration: Approximately 1 week.

1. Preparation Phase Check which servers are available and contact their presidents.
2. Invitation Phase Presidents can invite players based on their tier (Regular, Medium, Advanced, Elite).
3. Free Migration Phase
Regular tier players can migrate without an invitation if spots are available.

🎮 Requirements

  • You need migration tickets (amount depends on your power).
  • Players are categorized by tiers (Regular, Medium, Advanced, Elite).
  • Power calculation: Divide your first troop's power by 5 to get your approximate score.
  • You cannot migrate if you already migrated in the same round, are in combat, or are a president.

💡 Tips

• Negotiate with the president of the target server before the event.
- Get migration tickets in advance (missions/store).
- Evaluate the server carefully: migrate with a solid community rather than going alone to a weak server.

Popular Events

Reward events where you spend diamonds, tokens or real money

Shooting Loot

🎯 What is it?

Event where you spend Shooting Bullets to shoot at the target and earn rewards. Getting the grand prize of a round advances you to the next one with better treasures.

🎮 How It Works

Spend Shooting Bullets to shoot at the target. Before each round, choose the grand prize you want (you can change it during the event). The first 15 shots of each Regular Round guarantee the grand prize.

Special Rounds: Every round that is a multiple of 5 (5, 10, 15...) is a Treasure Round with better rewards. Limit: Maximum 500 rounds per event.

Notable Rewards:
• Round 3, 5, 12: Special rewards
• Round 12: Armor S Box (essential for F2P!)

💡 F2P Tips

• Main goal: Reach Round 12 for the Armor S Box.
• Buy the 100 shots with diamonds (10,000 diamonds = 100 shots) - best deal.
• Complete all event missions for free shots.
• Use Auto-Shot to save time.
• Choose as grand prize: Speed-ups or Cores.
• If you pass Round 12 and have shots left, save them for the next event.

🔄 Getting Bullets

Event tasks, purchase with diamonds, store packages. Unused bullets are saved for the next event.

Snowman Fun

⏰ Season 3 exclusive event

❄️ What is it?

Seasonal event where you collect Snowballs and Snowflakes to level up your Snowman and unlock valuable rewards.

🎮 How It Works

Snowstorm: A random storm appears each day. Your Shelter accumulates snow layers (max 5) that you must clear.

Getting Snowballs:
• Send drones to help 5 daily allies clear snow (you get free Balls).
• Complete 5 daily seasonal missions. Wait 10 min to claim or ask allies for help. ⚠️ Unclaimed rewards take up next-day attempts.

Getting Snowflakes: Defeat monsters each day.

Leveling Up: Spend Balls and Flakes to increase the snowman's EXP. Leveling up unlocks advanced forms and better rewards.

💡 Tips

• Help 5 allies and complete the 5 daily missions without fail.
• Ask allies for help to claim rewards faster.
• Don't leave rewards unclaimed.
• Farm monsters constantly to accumulate Snowflakes.

Lucky Discount Shop

📋 Rules

1. During the event, using discount coupons lets you draw a discount and unlock the shop for a limited time.
2. While the shop is active, products will have reduced prices based on the discount obtained. Once closed, items can no longer be purchased.
3. Each time you draw a discount, the shop resets, offering a new batch of products.
4. During the event, when refreshing discounts, you can get reductions of 90%, 70%, 50%, 30% or 10%.
5. Discount probabilities: 90% - 4%, 70% - 8%, 50% - 13%, 30% - 30%, 10% - 45%.
6. During the event, on the 5th, 9th, 18th and 32nd draw, a 90% discount is guaranteed.
7. When this event round ends, ranking rewards will be sent via mailbox.

💡 Tips

• If you don't have many diamonds, only buy at 90% or 70% discount.
• Wait until the last day so you have a chance to save more diamonds and use saved coupons.
• Spend your diamonds only if you get 70% or 90% discount.
• Claim the free coupon and the 2 daily coupons given through missions.
• The guaranteed draws (5th, 9th, 18th, 32nd) are the best buying opportunities.

Lucky Lucky Lucky

🎰 What is it?

Gacha event where you use diamonds or free daily attempts to spin a capsule machine and win chest keys, rare items or colored chests.

🎮 How It Works

Each draw launches balls that fall into 4 slots. There are two types of rewards:

Direct Item: 🔑 2 (18.4%) · 🔑 5 (8.18%) · 🔑 20 (1.02%) · 🔑 50 (0.02%) · ⭐ Badge (0.41%) · 📦 Chest x20 (10.22%)

Colored Chest: Goes to the depot (max. 100). You have only 20 minutes to open it with keys. Contains blueprints, speedups, golden keys and up to 60,000 blueprints in golden chests.

💰 Cost

• 1 Draw = 100 💎 · 5 Draws = 500 💎
• 5 free daily attempts
• Unused keys are saved for the next event

💡 F2P Tips

• Use the 5 free daily attempts without fail.
• Have keys ready before drawing — you only have 20 min to open the chests.
• Keep the depot below 100 to keep receiving chests.
• Accumulate keys between events to maximize openings.

Dawn's Light

☀️ What is it?

Daily event where you complete missions to earn Dawn Points and unlock the Hope Treasure upon reaching certain point milestones.

🎯 Daily Missions

• Complete radar events: 10 → 2pts / 20 → 5pts
• Train soldiers: 50 → 2pts / 100 → 5pts
• Eliminate giant zombies: 10 → 2pts / 20 → 5pts
• Challenge the arena: 2 times → 2pts / 5 times → 5pts

⚠️ Missions reset at 00:00 Apocalypse Time. Claim your rewards before that time.

🎁 Milestone Rewards

• 25 pts: Research speedup x60 (5min) · 🔑 2 chest keys · 1,000 blue blueprints
• 50 pts: 5 Advanced Recruit Cards · 🔑 2 chest keys · 1,000 blue blueprints
• 75 pts: Research speedup x60 (5min) · 🔑 2 chest keys · 1,000 blue blueprints
• 90 pts: 5 Advanced Recruit Cards · 🔑 2 grey keys · 1,000 blue blueprints
• 110 pts: 🔧 x10 Golden Keys · 🔑 2 chest keys · 1,000 blue blueprints

💡 F2P Tips

• Complete all daily missions — they're easy and quick.
• Always aim for 110 points — the Golden Keys from the last milestone are the best reward.
• The chest keys earned here work directly for Lucky Lucky Lucky.
• Don't forget to claim before 00:00 or you'll lose that day's progress.

VIP Missions

Exclusive missions for VIP players that grant premium rewards and special benefits based on your VIP level.

Tips

• Complete daily missions to maximize rewards.
• Review the benefits of each VIP level before investing.
• Take advantage of VIP experience bonuses during events.

Season Pass

Level-based reward system during a full season. Free and premium versions available with exclusive rewards.

Tips

• Calculate whether you can complete the pass before buying it.
• The premium version offers more than double the rewards.
• Complete daily and weekly missions to progress faster.
• Buy the pass at the start to take full advantage of all rewards.

Flash Sale

Very time-limited offers (1-24 hours) with significant discounts on premium packages, resources or heroes.

Tips

• Enable notifications so you don't miss the best deals.
• Compare the real discount value before impulse buying.
• Flash sales usually repeat, don't feel pressured.
• Save diamonds for truly exceptional deals.

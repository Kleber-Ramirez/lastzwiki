❄️ Season 3: Snow Monsters

The frozen tundra has arrived in Last Z. Face frozen zombies, snow monsters and master the eternal cold.

⏱️ ~70 days 🗺️ Snow Map ❄️ Boomers + Yeti 💎 Max level: 30

📋 Introduction

Season 3 maintains a similar structure to Season 2, but focuses much more on raids against snow bosses. The map transforms into a frozen wasteland where new enemies and challenges await.

🎯 Key Features:

  • New enemies: Snow Zombies (Boomers), Snow Leopard, Wandering Yeti
  • Exclusive seasonal buildings with unique resources
  • Specialization system: Support vs Combat (levels 1–30)
  • Snowstorm rescue events
  • Alliance ranking with 7 reward categories

🧬 Biological Laboratory PRIORITY #1

Function: Increases your virus resistance, allowing you to attack higher-level Boomers.

Reward: Boomers grant Ice Cores, required to upgrade ALL seasonal buildings.

Strategy: The most important building — upgrade it without hesitation. If you're a bit short on Lotus, wait; if very short, upgrade the Control Center first.

🌸 Temperature Control Center PRIORITY #2

Function: Produces Lotus Flowers, the main resource for upgrading seasonal buildings.

Strategy: If you have enough Ice Cores, upgrade it to increase Lotus production.

⚔️ Glacial Military Depot

Function: Increases your troops' ATTACK (ATK).

When to upgrade: After having the Bio Lab and Control Center at a good level.

🛡️ Glacial Fortress

Function: Increases your troops' DEFENSE.

When to upgrade: Alongside Military Depot to balance offense/defense.

💥 Military Command

Function: Increases your troops' DAMAGE.

When to upgrade: For players focused on combat and war.

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Individual rewards will be granted based on seasonal resources stored at season end. Don't waste them!

📦 Key Seasonal Resources:

🌸 Lotus Flowers

Produced in the Temperature Control Center. Required to upgrade the Biological Laboratory and other buildings.

❄️ Ice Cores

Obtained by eliminating Boomers. CRITICAL — needed to upgrade ALL seasonal buildings.

🎯 Upgrade Strategy (First 4 Weeks):

  1. Biological Laboratory — Top priority. Upgrade it constantly.
  2. Temperature Control Center — Second priority if you have spare Cores.
  3. Once you have good Lotus and Core production, upgrade the power buildings (Depot, Fortress, Command).

🎓 Specialization Room

Shared between Season 2 and Season 3. Choose your path based on your level and playstyle.

🔀 Choice System:

  • Level <30: You will be SUPPORT by default.
  • Level 30+: You can choose between SUPPORT or COMBAT.

🤝 Specialization: SUPPORT

Recommended for: All players. Maximizes individual progress and helps the team.

🛡️ Friendship Shield

Protects a teammate who is about to be attacked by the enemy.

Cooldown: Once every 3 days.

Strategy: Save it for war days — protect key members.

💎 Treasure Master

Summons a chest with resources and increases your carry capacity by 30%.

Limit: 2 attacks per day.

Strategy: Attack it twice with your first troop to maximize resources.

🏗️ Cooperative Construction

Reduces a teammate's construction time.

Cooldown: Once per day.

🔬 Cooperative Research

Reduces a teammate's research time.

Cooldown: Once per day.

⚔️ Specialization: COMBAT

Recommended for: Level 30+ players with hero power above 48 million.

🪖 Emergency Recruitment

Instantly recruits 150 soldiers.

Cooldown: Once per day. Úsalo en el evento "Preparación Total".

🎭 Disguise

Enemies won't get information when scouting you for 1 hour.

Strategy: Useful before an attack to hide your real power.

⚡ Quick Attack

Your focused attack departs faster.

Limit: 5 times per week. Extremely useful on war days.

🔄 Immediate Return

Your troops return to base immediately.

Limit: 2 times per day. Useful to reposition or escape.

📚 How to Obtain Experience Books:

  1. 🏆 Specialization Pass ($20): The fastest method.
  2. 🏗️ Upgrade seasonal buildings: Each upgrade grants books.
  3. 🏙️ Attack cities: Occupying and defending gives constant rewards.
  4. ❄️ Kill Boomers: Daily raids add up points.
  5. ⚔️ Attack enemy HQ: During alliance war.

🎯 Raid System

Raids are the main focus of S3. You can only earn 15 rewards per day across all raids — prioritize correctly.

❄️ BOOMERS (Snow Zombies) PRIORITY #1

Reward: Ice Cores (double that of the Leopard).

Daily limit: 15 rewards.

Estrategia: After server reset, start by attacking 15 of these. They are the best source of Ice Cores.

Requisito: Upgrade your Biological Laboratory to attack higher levels.

🐆 SNOW LEOPARD — INTERMEDIATE

Reward: Resources and glory, but less than Boomers.

Estrategia: Only attack it for the first elimination reward after using your 15 Boomer rewards.

🦍 YETI (Wandering Monster) — ADVANCED

Level: Found from level 100 to 350 on the map.

Daily limit: Only 2 rewards per day.

Condición: Rewards are only granted if you participate in the final raid.

Dificultad: Many troops will end up in the hospital. Only if your alliance has enough power.

💡 CRITICAL TIP — Yeti: If the Yeti approaches to attack you, start a raid and cancel it immediately. This makes it take a different route and you avoid damage.

📊 Daily Priority Summary:

  1. 15x Boomers — Every day after server reset
  2. 1x Snow Leopard — First elimination only if you have spare time
  3. Yeti — Only if your alliance is strong and coordinates

🌨️ Special Events

❄️ Snowstorm Rescue

Duration: Days 1–3 of Alliance vs Alliance.

Cold waves appear on the map forming large and small ice sculptures with animals inside.

Daily limit: You can only claim 10 rewards per day.

🎯 Strategy: Look for LARGE sculptures only. Share their location in alliance chat. Explore quickly to break them before others.

🎁 Rewards for Breaking Sculptures:

🦣 MAMMOTH

Grants 2 fragments for an exclusive seasonal weapon.

🦌 DEER

Grants 300 badges.

Choose the animal that benefits you most based on your current needs (weapon vs badges).

⚔️ Glory War

The most important event of the season. The winner can steal season points from the loser. Defines your final ranking position and rewards.

🏆 Season Point Ranking

Alliance Season Points = Personal Glory (all members) + City Season Points

📊 How to Earn Points:

  • Personal Glory: First elimination of a Snow Leopard or Tundra Zombie.
  • Cities: Occupying a city generates points over time. Accumulated points are NOT lost when you leave.
  • Glory War: Winning allows you to steal points from the enemy.

💡 Tips to Maximize Progress

  • ✅ Always prioritize Biological Laboratory → Control Center → Power Buildings
  • ✅ Use your 15 daily rewards on Boomers to maximize Ice Cores
  • ✅ Coordinate with your alliance to share large sculpture locations
  • ✅ Save Friendship Shield and Quick Attack for war days
  • ✅ Occupy and defend cities to accumulate passive season points

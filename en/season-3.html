❄️ SEASON 3: SNOW MONSTERS

The frozen tundra has arrived in Last Z. Face frozen zombies, snow monsters and master the eternal cold.

⏱️ Duration: ~70 days | 🗺️ Map: Snow Theme

📋 Introduction

Season 3 maintains a similar structure to Season 2, but focuses much more on raids against snow bosses. The map transforms into a frozen wasteland where new enemies and challenges await.

🎯 Key Features:

  • New enemies: Snow Zombies (Boomers), Snow Leopard, Wandering Yeti
  • Exclusive seasonal buildings with unique resources
  • Improved specialization system (Support vs Combat)
  • Snowstorm rescue events
  • Alliance ranking with 7 reward categories

💰 Recommended Passes:

🏆 Season Pass

$25 USD

Benefits for the full 70 days. Upgraded version available for $25+ with additional rewards.

📅 Weekly Pass

$10 USD

Weekly benefits. If you buy it 4 times during the season, you earn permanent bonuses.

🏗️ Season 3 Exclusive Buildings

These buildings are only available during Season 3 and use special seasonal resources.

🧬 Biological Laboratory PRIORITY #1

Function: Increases your virus resistance, allowing you to attack higher-level Boomers (Snow Zombies).

Reward: Boomers grant Ice Cores, required to upgrade ALL seasonal buildings.

Strategy: This is the MOST IMPORTANT building. Upgrade it without hesitation. If you're short on Lotus Flowers, wait; if you're very short, upgrade the Control Center first.

🌸 Temperature Control Center PRIORITY #2

Function: Produces Lotus Flowers, the main resource for upgrading seasonal buildings.

Strategy: If you have enough Ice Cores, upgrade it to increase Lotus production.

⚔️ Glacial Military Depot

Function: Increases your troops' ATTACK (ATK).

When to upgrade: After having the Biological Lab and Control Center at a good level.

🛡️ Glacial Fortress

Function: Increases your troops' DEFENSE.

When to upgrade: Alongside Military Depot to balance offense/defense.

💥 Military Command

Function: Increases your troops' DAMAGE.

When to upgrade: For players focused on combat and war.

⚠️ IMPORTANT: According to external information, individual rewards will be granted based on the amount of seasonal resources stored at the end.

📊 Key Seasonal Resources:

  • 🌸 Lotus Flowers: Produced in the Temperature Control Center. Required to upgrade the Biological Laboratory and other buildings.
  • ❄️ Ice Cores: Obtained by eliminating Boomers. CRITICAL because they are needed to upgrade ALL seasonal buildings.

🎯 Upgrade Strategy (First 4 Weeks):

  1. Biological Laboratory — Top priority. Upgrade it constantly.
  2. Temperature Control Center — Second priority if you have spare Cores.
  3. Once you have good Lotus and Core production, upgrade the power buildings (Depot, Fortress, Command).

🎓 Specialization Room

This building is shared between Season 2 and Season 3. Here you choose your specialization path based on your level and playstyle.

🔀 Choice System:

  • Level <30: You will be SUPPORT by default (no choice).
  • Level 30+: You can choose between SUPPORT or COMBAT.

🤝 Specialization: SUPPORT

Recommended for: All alliance players. Greatly increases your individual progress and helps the team.

🛡️ Friendship Shield

Protects a teammate who is about to be attacked by the enemy.

Cooldown: Once every 3 days.

Strategy: Use it quickly and wisely. Save it for war days and protect key members.

💎 Treasure Master

Summons a chest that grants resources when attacked. Also increases your carry capacity by 30%.

Limit: You can only attack it 2 times per day.

Strategy: Attack it twice using your first troop to maximize resources.

🏗️ Cooperative Construction

Reduces a teammate's construction time.

Cooldown: Once per day.

Strategy: Use it wisely on construction days to help members upgrading important buildings.

🔬 Cooperative Research

Reduces a teammate's research time.

Cooldown: Once per day.

Strategy: Similar to Cooperative Construction. Use it on research days to accelerate team progress.

⚔️ Specialization: COMBAT

Recommended for: Only level 30+ players with hero power above 48 million. Greatly increases your battle power.

🪖 Emergency Recruitment

Instantly recruits 150 soldiers.

Cooldown: Once per day.

Strategy: Best used when it coincides with the "Full Preparation" event to maximize benefits.

🎭 Disguise

Enemies won't get information when scouting you for 1 hour.

Duration: 1 hour.

Strategy: Very useful if you don't want enemies to know your resources or real power before an attack.

⚡ Quick Attack

Makes your focused attack depart faster.

Limit: Up to 5 times per week.

Reset: On vehicle upgrade day.

Strategy: Extremely useful on war days. Use it to hit key targets before the enemy can react.

🔄 Immediate Return

Makes your troops return to base immediately.

Limit: 2 times per day.

Strategy: Use it to enable a random teleport and escape, or to quickly reposition troops for a counterattack.

📚 How to Obtain Experience Books (Ranking):

Experience Books are needed to upgrade specialization skills. Here they are from most to least:

  1. 🏆 Buy Specialization Pass ($20): The fastest and most effective method.
  2. 🏗️ Upgrade seasonal buildings: Each upgrade grants books.
  3. 🏙️ Attack cities: Occupying and defending cities gives constant rewards.
  4. ❄️ Kill Boomers: Daily raids add up points.
  5. ⚔️ Attack enemy headquarters: During alliance war.

🎯 Raid System

Raids are the main focus of Season 3. You can only earn 15 rewards per day across all raids combined, so it's crucial to prioritize correctly.

❄️ BOOMERS (Snow Zombies) — PRIORITY #1

Reward: Ice Cores (double that of the Leopard).

Daily limit: 15 rewards.

Strategy: After server reset, start by attacking 15 of these. They are the best source of Ice Cores.

Requirement: Upgrade your Biological Laboratory to be able to attack higher levels.

🐆 SNOW LEOPARD — INTERMEDIATE

Reward: Resources and glory, but less than Boomers.

Strategy: Only attack it to claim the first elimination reward after using your 15 Boomer rewards.

🦍 YETI (Wandering Snow Monster) — ADVANCED

Level: Found from level 100 to 350 on the map.

Daily limit: You can only earn 2 rewards per day.

Condition: Rewards are only granted if you participate in the final raid (last hit).

Difficulty: Many troops will end up in the hospital. Only recommended if your alliance has enough power.

Recommended levels: For mid-power alliances, attack Yetis at level 120-180.

Rewards: Good, but Boomer rewards are enough for most players.

💡 CRITICAL TIP: If the Yeti approaches to attack you or a teammate, start a raid and cancel it immediately. This will make the Yeti take a different route and you'll avoid damage.

📊 Priority Summary:

  1. 15x Boomers (Snow Zombies) — Every day after reset
  2. 1x Snow Leopard — First elimination only if you have spare time
  3. Yeti — Only if your alliance is strong and coordinates

🌨️ Special Events

❄️ Snowstorm Rescue

Duration: Days 1-3 of Alliance vs Alliance.

Mechanic: Cold waves appear at different map locations, forming large and small ice sculptures with animals inside.

Daily limit: You can only claim 10 rewards per day during the event.

🎯 Strategy:

  • Look for LARGE sculptures only: Small ones are not worth it.
  • Share locations: When you find a large sculpture, share it in the alliance chat.
  • Explore quickly: This will break the sculpture faster and release the animal.

🎁 Rewards for Breaking Sculptures:

🦣 MAMMOTH:

Grants 2 fragments for an exclusive seasonal weapon.

🦌 DEER:

Grants 300 badges.

Decision: Choose the animal that benefits you most based on your needs (weapon vs badges). Remember to share the location in chat so your alliance can also benefit.

⚔️ Glory War

This event is key for the season ranking. During the final battle against the enemy alliance, the winner can steal season points from the loser.

Importance: Defines your final position in the ranking and the rewards you'll receive at the end of the season.

🏆 Season Point Ranking

📊 Points System:

Formula:

Alliance Season Points = Personal Glory Value (all members) + City Season Points

How to Earn Points:

  • Personal Glory: Earned with the first elimination of a Snow Leopard or Tundra Zombie.
  • Cities:
    • Occupying a city directly increases season points.
    • You generate points over time while you occupy it.
    • Points accumulated over time are NOT lost when you leave the city.
  • Glory War: Winning the final battle allows you to steal points from the enemy.

💡 General Tips to Maximize Progress

  • ✅ Always prioritize Biological Laboratory → Control Center → Power Buildings
  • ✅ Use your 15 daily rewards on Boomers to maximize Ice Cores
  • ✅ Coordinate with your alliance to share large sculpture locations
